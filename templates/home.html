The Study

Background on the Study

This goal of this study was to see if there was a correlation between Google trends data and the amount of money that a movie made. The idea for this project came to me after reading Everybody Lies. In that book the author uses data from Google Trends to examine human behvaior. Thus, I wanted to see if there would be any correlation with movies and Google Trends.

Methods Used

  • Gather Data on how much a 2016 movie made.
  • Use Google Trends data on the movie released. Take data from the point that the movie was released to one year before hand.
  • Get the mean of the Google Trends data for the past year.
  • Create scatter plot of data for using Trends data as X axis and amount movie made as Y axis.

The Results-One Year

Results cannot be seen on small screens

As we can see by the year results, there seems to be no real correlation between the amount that a movie earned and its Google trends value. Some movies, that earned a lot of money, such as Rogue One, had a low Google Trends value. Other movies, with a high Google trends value, barely earned 100 Million at the box office. Thus, at this moment, I would argue that there is no correlation, at the one year mark, between Google Trends and the amount of money a movie made.
